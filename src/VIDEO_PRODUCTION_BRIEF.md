# ChemoVigi Video Production Brief

## Project Overview
Create a professional medical animation video showcasing ChemoVigi's AI-powered pharmacovigilance platform for adverse drug reaction (ADR) reporting.

## Reference Video Style
Based on: https://vimeo.com/652027664
- **Style**: Professional medical/pharmaceutical 3D animation
- **Tone**: Clean, modern, scientific, trustworthy
- **Visual Quality**: High-end medical visualization
- **Duration Target**: 60-90 seconds

---

## Video Concept: "The Journey of an Adverse Drug Reaction Report"

### Scene 1: The Problem (0-15 seconds)
**Visual Elements:**
- Start with a 3D rotating DNA helix/molecular structure (similar to reference video)
- Transition to animated chemotherapy drugs flowing through bloodstream
- Show red warning indicators appearing around cells
- Display statistics floating in 3D space:
  - "1.3M+ Emergency Visits Annually"
  - "94% of ADRs Go Unreported"
  - "45-46% Are Preventable"

**Narration/Text Overlay:**
"Adverse Drug Reactions cause over 1.3 million emergency visits annually. Most go unreported. Until now."

**Color Scheme:** Blue-to-red gradient showing danger/concern

---

### Scene 2: Patient Reports (15-30 seconds)
**Visual Elements:**
- 3D smartphone/tablet materializes from particles
- Patient avatar (silhouette) interacting with ChemoVigi interface
- Natural language message appears: "I'm experiencing severe nausea and dizziness"
- Message transforms into digital data particles
- ChemoVigi logo appears with branding colors (blue, teal, green)

**Narration/Text Overlay:**
"ChemoVigi makes reporting simple. Just describe your symptoms in your own words."

**Color Scheme:** Transition to hopeful blues and teals

---

### Scene 3: AI Analysis (30-50 seconds)
**Visual Elements:**
- Data particles flow into a glowing AI brain/neural network
- 3D molecular structures rotate and get analyzed
- Multiple data points light up showing:
  - Severity scoring (WHO criteria)
  - Drug classification
  - Pattern recognition across 500,000+ cases
- Speed indicators showing "Analysis complete in 2 seconds"
- Beautiful particle effects showing data processing

**Narration/Text Overlay:**
"Our AI instantly analyzes severity using WHO criteria and 500,000+ case database."

**Color Scheme:** Purple and blue neural network glow

---

### Scene 4: Clinical Action (50-70 seconds)
**Visual Elements:**
- Alert notification materializes in 3D space
- Clinician avatar receives priority-coded report
- Show professional dashboard with patient data (anonymized)
- Medical professional reviewing case on modern interface
- Connection lines between patient → AI → clinician
- Rwanda FDA logo appears as a trusted partner seal

**Narration/Text Overlay:**
"Clinicians receive instant, AI-prioritized alerts for immediate action."

**Color Scheme:** Professional greens showing resolution/safety

---

### Scene 5: Global Impact (70-85 seconds)
**Visual Elements:**
- Zoom out to show globe/Earth
- Multiple connection points lighting up (representing reports)
- Data flowing between hospitals, patients, regulatory bodies
- Growth graph showing increasing safety
- 3D molecules stabilizing (representing safer drug use)
- Statistics appear:
  - "847% increase in reports"
  - "99.2% AI accuracy"
  - "Zero critical cases missed"

**Narration/Text Overlay:**
"Making drug safety better for everyone, everywhere."

**Color Scheme:** Global blue-green gradient

---

### Scene 6: Call to Action (85-90 seconds)
**Visual Elements:**
- ChemoVigi logo assembles from DNA particles
- Website URL materializes: www.chemovigi.com
- Tagline appears in elegant typography
- Particle effects creating professional medical atmosphere

**Narration/Text Overlay:**
"ChemoVigi: Improving Drug Safety Through Better Reporting"

**Color Scheme:** Full brand gradient (blue → teal → green)

---

## Technical Specifications

### Visual Style Requirements
1. **3D Medical Animations:**
   - Molecular structures (DNA, proteins, drug molecules)
   - Neural network visualizations
   - Particle effects for data flow
   - Smooth camera movements
   - Professional medical-grade rendering

2. **Color Palette:**
   - Primary: Blue (#3B82F6)
   - Secondary: Teal (#14B8A6)
   - Accent: Green (#22C55E)
   - Warning/Problem: Red (#EF4444)
   - Background: Clean white/light gradients

3. **Typography:**
   - Clean, modern sans-serif
   - Bold for statistics
   - Professional medical aesthetic
   - Easy to read

4. **Motion Design:**
   - Smooth transitions between scenes (2-3 seconds)
   - Particle effects for data visualization
   - Rotating 3D elements
   - Professional pacing (not too fast)

### Audio Requirements
1. **Background Music:**
   - Professional, modern medical/tech feel
   - Uplifting and hopeful
   - Not overpowering narration
   - Build intensity during AI analysis scene

2. **Sound Effects:**
   - Subtle "whoosh" for transitions
   - Digital/tech sounds for data processing
   - Notification sounds for alerts
   - No harsh or jarring sounds

3. **Narration (Optional):**
   - Professional voiceover (male or female)
   - Calm, trustworthy, authoritative tone
   - Clear enunciation
   - Medical professional credibility
